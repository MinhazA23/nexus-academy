About Us

Creating World Champions

Nexus Academy is the largest global esports institution, known for its high-quality training programs and modern facilities. It offers a range of courses taught by experienced coaches and hosts tournaments and events. Its commitment to excellence and dedication to the esports community make it a top destination for those looking to elevate their professional career. Bugha attended Nexus Academy, where he excelled thanks to the top-of-the-line facilities, expert coaches, and rigorous training schedule. He earned a spot on the school's competitive team and, together, they dominated local and regional tournaments. Bugha's consistent performance throughout the year earned him the 2019 esports player of the year award. He credits Nexus Academy for his success and vows to continue representing the school with pride.
